How to use:-
Follow instructions

Setup:-
1. Open your Discord server.
2. Select the Channel where you want to send your messages.
3. click on the gears icon (Edit Channel).
4. Go to integretions>Webhooks>New Webhook.
5. Name and edit the bot as you wish.
6. Click on 'Copy Webhook URL'.
7. Open the code in compiler.
8. Press 1.
9. Paste the Url, Type your messages and hit enter.
10. The message will be sent in the channel!
